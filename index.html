About

Ever since I developed my first Windows Phone App during the final year of my Bachelor studies (i.e. Computer Science & Engineering), I have a passion for developing Apps that stay in aligning with mobile usability guidelines. In December 2017, I graduated with a master's degree in (M.Sc.) Computer Science from Technische Universitaet Berlin. My master thesis title: "Review on continuous integration in mobile development."

Software Engineer - Mobile, specializing in native mobile apps and had written in C#, Java, Swift. I have created apps for Windows Phone, Windows 8, Android, iOS that follow human interface guidelines and fit into their respective ecosystems with distinct branding.

Software Engineer - Mobile with an experience of 5+ years
