Introduction

I'm Application developer (i.e., Windows Phone, Windows 8, Android), specializing in native apps. Ever since I built my first Windows Phone app during final year of my Bachelor studies(i.e., Computer Science & Engineering), I have a passion for developing apps.

I have created apps for Windows Phone, Windows 8, Android that follow Microsoft Corporation Metro & Android design guidelines and fit into Windows & Android ecosystem with distinct branding.

I'm also GIS Engineer & Cartographer, through various projects [e.g., making maps of Berlin through SAR imagery, Data Visualization of Berlin houses by consumption of household resources] & courses undertaken during my Master studies (i.e., Geodesy and Geoinformation Science)
