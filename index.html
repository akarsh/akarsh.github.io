About

Ever since I developed my first Windows Phone App during the final year of my Bachelor studies (i.e. Computer Science & Engineering), I have a passion for developing Apps that stay in aligning with mobile usability guidelines. In December 2017, I graduated with a master's degree in (M.Sc.) Computer Science from Technische Universitaet Berlin. My master thesis title: "Review on continuous integration in mobile development."

IT Team Lead, initiating, developing and implementing strategies for internal/external software to improve the operational process and overall productivity of different teams in the organization.

Previous experience as Software Engineer - Mobile, specializing in native mobile apps and had written in C#, Java, Swift. I have created apps for Windows Phone, Windows 8, Android, iOS that follow human interface guidelines and fit into their respective ecosystems with distinct branding.

IT Team Lead with an experience of 10+ years
